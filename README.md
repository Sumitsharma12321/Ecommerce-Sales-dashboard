
# 📊 E-commerce Sales Dashboard – Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a comprehensive overview of e-commerce sales performance, including revenue, profit, customer behavior, and product category insights.

---

## 🖼️ Dashboard Preview

![E-commerce Sales Dashboard](./1774609053305_image.png)

---

## 📌 Overview

This dashboard is built using **Microsoft Power BI** and visualizes key business metrics from an e-commerce dataset. It enables stakeholders to quickly understand sales trends, customer spending patterns, and category-wise performance across different time periods and regions.

---

## 📊 Visualizations Included

### 1. 🗺️ Amount by State
A horizontal bar chart showing total sales amount across Indian states:
- **Maharashtra** – highest revenue
- Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, and Delhi also featured

### 2. 🍩 Quantity by Category
A donut chart breaking down product quantity sold by category:
- **Clothing** – 63%
- **Electronics** – 21%
- **Furniture** – 17%

### 3. 📅 Profit by Month
A bar chart displaying monthly profit trends throughout the year, with both positive and negative profit months highlighted (blue = profit, orange = loss).

### 4. 👤 Most Spend Customer
A bar chart ranking the top customers by total spend:
- Harivansh, Madhav, Madan Mohan, Shiva

### 5. 💳 Quantity by Payment Mode
A donut chart showing payment method distribution:
- **COD** – 44%
- **UPI** – 21%
- **Debit Card** – 13%
- **Credit Card** – 12%
- **EMI** – 10%

### 6. 🏷️ Profit by Sub-Category
A horizontal bar chart showing profit contributions by product sub-category:
- Printers, Bookcases, Saree, Accessories, Tables

---

## 🔍 Filters & Interactivity

- **Quarter Filter** – Toggle between Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4 for time-period analysis
- **State/Category Slicer** – Filter data by region or product category
- All visuals are cross-filtered and interactive
